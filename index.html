We love Barcelona

The nickname "culer" for Barcelona fans comes from the Catalan word "cul" (English: butt, ass), when spectators at the first stadium, Camp de la Indústria, sat with "cul" in the stands. In Spain, around 25% of the population is said to support Barça, second only to Real Madrid at 32%. Across Europe, Barcelona is the second most popular club.
